Why Mycelium-Based Packaging?

Thermocol (EPS) poses a major threat to our environment—it takes nearly 2000 years to degrade and harms marine life. Our solution is a revolutionary packaging material made from mushroom mycelium—biodegradable, compostable, lightweight, and strong.
